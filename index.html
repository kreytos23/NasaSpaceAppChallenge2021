Tezcatlipoca's Team

We are the Tezcatlipoca's team. Participating in the Nasa Space App Challenge 2021. We chose the challenge Drones and Satellites for Urban Development. Our solution is based on the development of a web application for the analysis of possibilities for the construction of solar panel farms or wind generators.

Why this challenge?

Currently, the state of Puebla has been reporting excessive energy demands, to the point where there have been energy shortages. This is why we must opt for better sources of energy, besides the ecological brand is reaching a point of no return, by opting for environmentally friendly energy this will help climate problems, while helping communities at risk where electricity does not reach properly.
